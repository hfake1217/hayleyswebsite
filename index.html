About Me

I am physical therapist turned software engineer. I grew up in Harrisburg, Pennsylvania with a passion for health and physical activity. I graduated from University of Pittsburgh in 2016 with a degree in Exercise Science, and then went on to obtain my Doctorate of Physical Therapy at University of Maryland - Easten Shore in 2019. I moved to Tampa, Florida in 2020, where I am currently residing, and plan to settle here with my fiance, Clay.

I have been working as an outpatient physical therapist since 2019, with a sports focus since January of 2022. Over the last year or so, I have become more interested in healthcare technology and increasing accessibility to individuals with a goal of improving their fitness and health or maintaining a healthy well-being. This is when I became more intereseted in breaking into the tech industry. My goal is to combine my passion for health and fitness with my new interest for technology to transform the way individuals manage their fitness.

To start my journey breaking into tech, I have completed the Complete 2023 Web Development Bootcamp by Angela Yu on Udemy, and I have also completed the freeCodeCamp Responsive Web Design certification. With these certifications, I am a full-stack web developer and have covered topics including HTML, CSS, JavaScript, node.js, express, MongoDB, and React.
